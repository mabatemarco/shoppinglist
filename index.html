What is Shopping List?

Shopping List is a tool to make the planning, shopping, and cooking of meals easy. Don't know what to make this week? Choose from a random selection of meals that meet your criterea and taste. Worried about buying too much or too little at the store? Shopping List gives you precise amounts to make sure you can prepare your week's meals without waste or falling short. Enjoying food shouldn't be stressful. Plan your meals, shop your list, and follow along with the recipes.

How does it work?

Shopping List is easy. Select your meal preferences and restrictions, press go, and we'll provide a selection of meals for you to choose from. Select the meals that look good to you, press finish, and you'll get a shopping list and links to detailed recipes for your meals. Press the button below to get started.
